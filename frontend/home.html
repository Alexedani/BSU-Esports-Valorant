About this tool

What is this tool?

Tracks Valorant ranked data for listed players and sends weekly stats to our Google Sheet.

How to use it

  1. Add players by name + tag (e.g., master / bsu).
  2. Click Run Scrape Now and watch the terminal.
  3. When done, click View Results to open the sheet.

Where are results?

Open the Google Sheet

Need help?

Contact the site admin.

Notes

  • You can scroll the terminal to view recent logs.
  • The tracked players table persists on the server; it may reset on app updates.
